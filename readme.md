# 1. Title: Swiftie Verse
## 2. Discription: This website to reach all swifties worldwide to better understand their favorite artist's, Taylor Swift's, journey to her musical career success. It aims to compile all of Taylor Swift's albums and their history so that her fans and even those just discovering her music are able to appreciate not just her music itself, but the history behind it. 
## 3. This is for all swities or anyone who appreciates Taylor's music. It will involve classic hits during her career up until her latest album, The Life of a Showgirl. The Swiftie Verse aims to be a safe place for swifties to enjoy and explore the world of Taylor Swift to learn a little more about her. It includes not only her career, but also a little into her personal life like her hobbies and many more (without any invasion of her privacy ofcourse). Despite mass media often ridiculing Taylor's career, and fame and success, this project shows that it's okay to embrace thing you love, like her muisc. Taylor's music has helped many swities in many ways, such as a source of comfort, so we aim this website to be a "love letter" to everything she has done for us. 
## 4. They will love this project because of multiple reasons. Firstly, the website has Taylor's music that the users can play, which deeply connect them to the music of Taylor Swift. Secondly, through the interactive friendship bracelet and album game, there is interaction between the website and the users. Lastly, there is history and descriptions about Taylor Swift, which are able to help the users gain more appreciation for her.
## 5. This website will firstly feature a registration/sign-up to save your account, which will eventually lead you to various pages. The albums page includes all of her albums and their descriptions, even with one popular song from each album that can be played. At the top, there is a "which TS album are you?" game, which randomly generates each album when you click the button. On the other hand, the timeline page features each of Taylor's milestones in her career, from her first album to her world tour. Next, the concerts features particularly her Eras Tour, with pictures and posters of the events. There is also a friendship bracelet generator at the top, which virtually takes the user to the eras tour. Lastly, besides our sources, there is the About TS page. This includes Taylor's hobbies, lifestyle, and other relevant information about her life. The sources page introduces the creators and the sources we used to create the website.

It will have several features, showing how she made her music, the lyrics to her music, and several visual aids to show her beautiful artistry. As Swifties ourselves, we aim to make this website a comprehensive guide to Taylor's music for the audience.
******
## Webpage Breakdown:
### Home Page: The Home Page will include a description of the website's purpose and why we chose this theme. Additionally, it will contain a simple menu that will allow the user to go to each of the other 5 web pages. 
### P1 (Albums): This page will showcase Taylor Swift's 12 studio albums throughout her music career (from Debut to The Life of a Showgirl). In each album, it will discuss its unique characteristics and other related information.
### P2 (Timeline): This page will talk about the life of Taylor's career, like the awards, achievements, and challenges she faced to achieve the success she has now.
### P3 (Concerts): This page will present Taylor's most recent concerts, The Eras Tour. It will include information such as the duration, album, stage setup, countries, and fan experience. 
### P4 (About TS): Aside from Taylor's massive part in the music industry, this page will talk about her not as a pop singer, but also as a person. This page will talk about Taylor's hobbies, interests, pets, lifestyle, and current status.
### P5 (Sources): This page will contain the list of all the sources used for the project.
